  • The Company destroys personal information without delay when the retention period expires or the processing purpose is achieved.

8.2 Destruction Methods

Article 9 (Measures to Ensure Safety of Personal Information)

The Company takes the following measures to ensure the safety of personal information.

9.1 Administrative Measures

  • Establishment and implementation of internal management plans
  • Minimization of personal information handling staff and regular education
  • Regular self-inspection and internal auditing
  • Retention and inspection of access records to personal information processing systems (at least 6 months)
  • Establishment of response system for personal information leakage incidents

9.2 Technical Measures

A. Encryption of Personal Information

  • Passwords are stored using one-way encryption (hash functions)
  • Sensitive information such as resident registration numbers and account information is encrypted for storage and transmission
  • Security protocols such as SSL/TLS are applied when transmitting personal information

B. Technical Measures Against Hacking, etc.

  • Operation of firewalls and intrusion detection systems (IDS)
  • Installation and periodic updates of security programs (antivirus, malware detection tools)
  • Installation and operation of systems in areas with controlled external access

C. Access Control

  • Access rights management for personal information processing systems
  • Installation and operation of access control systems
  • Differential granting of access rights to sensitive information such as unique identification information
  • Access restrictions for unauthorized persons and retention of access records

9.3 Physical Measures

  • Access control to computer rooms, data storage rooms, etc.
  • Storage of documents and auxiliary storage media containing personal information in safe places with locking devices
  • CCTV installation and access record management

Article 10 (Operation of Automatic Collection Devices such as Cookies)

10.1 Purpose of Using Cookies

The Company uses cookies to provide better services to users. Cookies are small text files sent by a server operating a website to a user's browser, stored on the user's computer hard disk. The Company uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analysis of access frequency and visit time of members and non-members
  • Identification of users' tastes and areas of interest
  • Identification of degree of participation in various events and visit frequency
  • Provision of user-customized services and personalized content recommendations
  • Measures for target marketing and service improvement

10.2 Cookie Installation, Operation, and Refusal

A. Cookie Setting Methods

Users have the option to choose cookie installation. Users can set cookie permissions, cookie blocking, etc. through web browser option settings.

  • Internet Explorer: [Tools] > [Internet Options] > [Privacy] tab
  • Chrome: [Settings] > [Privacy and Security] > [Cookies and other site data]
  • Firefox: [Settings] > [Privacy and Security] > [Cookies and Site Data]
  • Safari: [Preferences] > [Privacy] > [Cookies and website data]

B. Impact of Cookie Refusal

If cookie storage is refused, there may be difficulties in using some services that require login, and provision of customized services may be restricted.

10.3 Other Automatic Collection Devices

The Company may use the following automatic collection devices in addition to cookies.

  • Mobile Device Identifiers (Device ID, ADID, etc.): App usage analysis and customized service provision
  • SDK (Software Development Kit): Service quality improvement and user experience analysis
  • Log Files: Service usage records, access logs, IP addresses, etc.

Users can restrict or reset advertising identifier use through mobile device settings.
